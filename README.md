## English

Filmix is more than a filter collection. It gives creators control over the variables of a film look. By simulating film grain, tonal response, color temperature, and optical character, Filmix aims to retain the texture and warmth of analog photography.

### Highlights

- A complete film-simulation system with professional color tools
- Flexible combinations of parameters and filters for personal film looks
- Custom presets that can be saved and shared with other creators
- Built for both everyday moments and deliberate visual storytelling
